Privacy Policy

AUH respects your privacy. This policy will help explain how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our website, sign up for our email list, or make purchases through our online store.

Information We Collect

We only collect information in 2 ways:

  1. Email Sign-Ups (via ConvertKit)

    When you subscribe to our mailing list, we collect your email address so we can send you updates about new releases, tours, and exclusive merch.

  2. Merchandise Sales (via Square)

    When you click our Store link, you're redirected to our online store powered by Square. Any information you provide there (like your name, address, or payment details) is handled securely by Square under their own privacy policies.

We do not collect or store your payment or shipping information on our own website.

How We Use Your Information

To send you email updates, announcements, and news about All Under Heaven (via ConvertKit).

To analyze email engagement (opens and clicks) so we can improve what we send you.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

ConvertKit for managing our email list. You can read their Privacy Policy here

These platforms may collect data through cookies or similar technologies. Please refer to their policies for full details.
