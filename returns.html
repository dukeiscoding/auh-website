Return Policy

We want you to love everything you get from all under heaven. If something’s off with your order — whether it arrived damaged, was the wrong item, or didn’t show up at all — please reach out and we’ll make it right.

Return Guidelines

Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery for damaged or incorrect items.

To be eligible for a return, items must be unused and in original condition.

We do not currently accept exchanges unless the item arrived defective.

Sale and limited edition items are final sale unless damaged in transit.

To start a return, contact us with your order number and a description of the issue.
